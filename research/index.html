Our Process

From question to publication.

All WYI outputs follow a structured production cycle designed to maintain quality, independence, and methodological transparency.

Structured cycle. Quality and independence guaranteed.

01

Topic Selection

Research teams identify issues with clear youth relevance and policy salience. Topics are reviewed and approved by WYI editorial leadership.

02

Research & Writing

Authors conduct structured research using academic sources, official data, and comparative case analysis following the format's methodology.

03

Editorial Review

Every submission undergoes a structured peer review for factual accuracy, analytical rigour, and clarity before proceeding to publication.

04

Open Publication

Approved outputs are published open-access on the WYI website and distributed to relevant policy networks and partner organisations.
