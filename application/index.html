Open Roles

Two ways to
contribute to WYC.

Whether you want to lead a national chapter or contribute analytical work to an international research team, we have a role for you. Both positions are voluntary and open to applicants worldwide.

Research Role

Researcher

Researchers are the analytical core of WYC. You conduct policy research within a national team, contribute to publications, and engage with an international network of peers.

Researchers are the analytical core of WYC. You conduct policy research, contribute to publications, and engage with an international network of peers.

Responsibilities
  • Conduct independent and collaborative policy research on assigned topics
  • Contribute to the drafting and editing of WYC policy briefs and working papers
  • Participate in regular team meetings and cross-national research exchanges
  • Review and provide feedback on research produced by fellow team members
  • Represent WYC's research standards in all written and public work
What You Gain
  • Published policy research with international reach
  • Access to WYC's global researcher network and events
  • WYC Research Certificate upon successful completion
  • Mentorship from senior researchers and academics
Ψ
Leadership Role

National Ambassador Ψ

Ambassadors are WYC's national representatives. You build and lead your country's research team, coordinate research activities, and serve as the primary liaison to the global WYC leadership.

Ambassadors are WYC's national representatives. You build and lead your country's research team and liaise with the global WYC leadership.

Responsibilities
  • Recruit, build, and manage a national research team of 3–8 researchers
  • Coordinate research activities and ensure deadlines and quality standards are met
  • Represent WYC at national, regional, and international policy forums and events
  • Liaise regularly with the WYC global leadership board on strategy and direction
  • Promote WYC's work and expand the organisation's presence in your country
  • Support researchers in their development and ensure team cohesion
What You Gain
  • Direct access to WYC's global leadership board
  • Speaking and representation opportunities at policy events
  • WYC Leadership Certificate upon successful completion
  • Leadership development and organisational management experience
